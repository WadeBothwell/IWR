Why Use IT’S ORK-RELATED

  • It’s free.
  • Create & customise your own digital Profile – detail your skills, qualifications, experience, achievements, portfolio work or personal services.
  • Let employers & clients find you.
  • Share your digital CV (Personal URL).
  • You decide which of your details are ‘Public’ or ‘Private’.

How It Works

  • Anyone can filter & search Profiles for the particular skills they are looking to employ.
  • Only your ‘Public’ Profile will be displayed in search results, contact details are confidential & will not be displayed.
  • ONLY registered users can ‘Request’ to view your ‘Private’ Profile & contact details.
  • You receive the request & the requester’s details. You decide who can see your ‘Private’ Profile & contact details.
  • Search results will be sorted according to most recently updated. So update your Profile regularly.
